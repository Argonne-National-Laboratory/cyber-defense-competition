About CCHC
 

 

The CyberInnovations Critical Hosting Company (CCHC) is a corporation centrally located in Metropolitan, Illinois. We function as a hosting company and have many critical infrastructure clients from around Illinois. Our clients are small power distributors with the responsibility to provide power to customers and homes. CCHC's clients have secure access to local storage of their own information, without incurring the overhead costs of maintaining an onsite information technology (IT) staff. CCHC provides Web, file sharing, and remote operating services for each physical site.

  
 
 

CCHC Services


At CCHC, we provide web services, file sharing, and remote operating services for each client's physical site. You can trust us to provide secure access to your information, while keeping your costs low. CCHC provides the following capabilities for clientele:

Web Services

CCHC Clientele have the ability to login to our Web server to update their Web content, taking the hassle out of hiring an onsite IT team.

File Sharing

CCHC Clientele can utilize our file server to upload and download content as needed. To learn more about this service, please reach out to us via any of the methods listed in the "Contact" tab.

Remote Operations

Perhaps our most sought after feature, CCHC Clientele are able to utilize our remote operations capabilities. CCHC provides secure access for command and control (C&C) of our client's associative supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) environment. Our experts at CCHC offer impeccable functionality securely into the environment, while providing required services and protecting this capability from compromise.

CCHC Security

 



For your business to succeed, customers need to trust that you'll protect them from viruses, hackers and identity thieves. Count on CCHC to keep your information secure, your data available, and your business growing.
